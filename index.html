our philosophy

Ancestral ingredients, modern small batches.

GROUNDED started from a simple frustration: most "natural" skin care still reads like a chemistry set. Ours doesn't. Every product is built from ingredients people have used for generations — nothing added because a marketing team thought it should be there.

We make everything in small batches, by hand, and we'd rather sell out of a scent than compromise on what goes into the bottle.
